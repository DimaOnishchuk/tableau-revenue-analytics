# Project requirements

## Business context

The project represents revenue analysis for a product company with customers across multiple locations.

The dashboards should help stakeholders understand:

- revenue dynamics over time;
- location and product performance;
- the number of paid users;
- average revenue per paid user;
- revenue from newly converted paying users;
- month-over-month revenue change;
- customer cohort behavior.

## Part 1 — Core revenue dashboards

### Basic worksheets

1. Monthly Revenue — line chart
2. Monthly Revenue by Location — multi-line chart
3. Total Revenue by Product — bar chart

### Calculated metrics

1. Total Revenue
2. Paid Users Count
3. Average Revenue Per Paid User

### Analytical worksheets

1. Revenue by Location & Software — clustered bar chart
2. Revenue by Location — bar chart
3. Revenue by Month & Software — multi-line chart
4. ARPPU & Paid Users Count — dual-axis chart

### Dashboard 1

- Monthly Revenue by Location
- Monthly Revenue
- Total Revenue by Product

### Dashboard 2

- Revenue by Location & Software
- Revenue by Month & Software
- ARPPU & Paid Users Count
- Revenue by Location

### Filters

- Location
- Software product
- Payment month

## Part 2 — Advanced revenue dashboard

### New MRR

A bar chart showing revenue generated during the calendar month in which each user first became a paying user.

### Total Revenue Difference

A dual-axis chart containing:

- Total Revenue as bars
- Month-over-month Revenue Change as a line

### Cohort Analysis

A cohort table where:

- rows contain the user's first payment month;
- columns contain the number of months since the first payment;
- cells display Total Revenue;
- color represents revenue change relative to the cohort's first month.

### Dashboard filters

- Location
- Payment date
